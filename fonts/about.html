Our Team

Provide professionals trained with the latest information

We have more than 25 years of experience in management and recruitment of people in Information Technology (IT). We are united by the desire to select technical experts in computer systems and business organizations to train them in the latest techniques in IT and dedicate them to meet the existing demand for this discipline in government companies, private entities, hospitals, and health centers.

It should be noted that CIDESA GROUP's team of systems analysts is made up of university professionals, specialists in information management, workflow, data management, software development, implementation of computer systems, and business organization. It reviews and adjusts your company's IT job allocation procedures, functions, and integration with other dependencies. It also provides consultancy to evaluate the processes of unification of the hardware and software platform of the companies.

More than 25 years of business management at the customer's side. Our success lies in part in implementing our interaction-action methodology designed to help determine the information needs of the position to be filled, its functions, customer requirements, and the integration of the company's IT procedures, both internal and external. We then review the information generated by this process and assign the appropriate technician based on their skills, aptitudes, and experience.
