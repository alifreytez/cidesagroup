Integrated Management Systems Audits

We offer expert consulting services for Integrated Management Systems Audits (IMS Audits) with qualified consultants who evaluate an organization's strategies, norms, policies, standards, and procedures to ensure the IT platform's operability and minimize fraud risks.

Our services also involve evaluating and analyzing the relationship between the information system and the organization to determine if it functions effectively, efficiently, and securely, including infrastructure, risks, and internal controls. We identify any weaknesses in the information system that may affect data integrity, confidentiality, or availability.

The consultancies cover various areas, such as update management, backup and disaster recovery, vulnerability identification, and recommendations to improve an organization's information system's security, efficiency, and effectiveness.

Our comprehensive consulting services include the following:

  • Reviewing the legal framework of the company's systems.
  • Examining the system's conceptual model, audit objectives, process flows, technology, priorities, resources, methodology, schedule, plans, impact, performance, management systems procedures, and scope.
  • Ensuring the validity of information, analyzing changes' impact on data integrity by reviewing documents and records, and conducting interviews with personnel.
  • Evaluating security and software quality.
  • Reviewing procedure manuals, standards, implementation procedures, and technical documentation.
  • Assessing process authenticity, interoperability, system confidentiality, modernization, flexibility, human growth, and organizational development.
  • Analyzing implementation procedures, computer networks, inputs, outputs, processes, interfaces, and application support.
  • Inspecting the software, strategic plan, functional objectives, goals, users, and technical staff.
  • Assessing cooperation between users and their participation in the resolution of critical problems.
